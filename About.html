Me

My name is Celia, I was born and raised in Spain. Following a Masters degree in Architecture and Creativity, I worked for a while in Slovenia, where I set my mind into becoming a designer. From there I found my way into digital and joined a UX/UI bootcamp. I am inspired by passionate people, long walks by the river, independent comic illustration, interior design and underground music. On my spare time I draw and have been doing yoga for a couple of years.

Top 3 graphic novels:
